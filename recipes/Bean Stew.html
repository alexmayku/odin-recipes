Description

A delightfully light, greek inspired twist on the middle eastern classic. Packed with vital energy and easy to prepare

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Feta
  • Kale
  • Cavolo Nero
  • Courgettes
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a pan, add in the garllic whilst the oil is still cold
  2. Heat to an upper-medium high heat, when the garlic begins to turn gold add the spinach
